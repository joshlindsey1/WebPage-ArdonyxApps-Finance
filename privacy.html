Privacy Policy

Transparent data handling for Ardonyx Finance.

This page explains, at a high level, what information Ardonyx Finance may process, how that information is used, and how users can reach support.

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Ardonyx Finance is a product experience operated under the Ardonyx Apps brand.

Information We May Collect

Depending on the feature a user authorizes, Ardonyx Finance may receive account, balance, transaction, and identity-related information from connected providers or financial institutions.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide personal finance functionality such as account visibility, transaction review, balance monitoring, and related user-requested insights.

User Authorization

Financial data is intended to be accessed only after a user explicitly authorizes a supported connection through a trusted aggregation or institution flow.

Credential Handling

Ardonyx Finance is not presented as a site for directly collecting banking credentials on this marketing domain. Where integrations are supported, connections are intended to occur through approved third-party or institution-hosted experiences.

Information Sharing

User information is not intended to be sold. Data may be shared only as necessary to operate requested services, comply with legal obligations, or protect platform security and integrity.

Support And Questions

Users can request support or ask privacy-related questions by emailing ArdonyxApps@gmail.com.

Important note

This site is a public-facing product presence for Ardonyx Finance. If you later add jurisdiction-specific requirements, retention timelines, or legal disclosures, this page should be expanded to reflect those details.
