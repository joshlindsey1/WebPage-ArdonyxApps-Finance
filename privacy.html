Privacy Policy

Privacy information for the Ardonyx Finance consumer application.

This page explains, at a high level, what information Ardonyx Finance may process after user authorization, how that information is used within the product, and how users can reach support.

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Ardonyx Finance is operated by Ardonyx Apps and presented as a consumer personal finance product.

Information We May Collect

Depending on the feature a user authorizes, Ardonyx Finance may receive account, balance, transaction, and limited identity-related information from supported connection providers or financial institutions.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide personal finance functionality requested by the user, including account visibility, transaction review, balance monitoring, and cash flow tracking within the Ardonyx Finance experience.

User Authorization

Financial data is accessed only after a user explicitly authorizes a supported connection through an approved aggregator flow or institution-provided authorization experience.

Credential Handling

This public website is not presented as a place for users to directly submit online banking credentials. Where connections are supported, account linking is intended to occur through approved third-party or institution-hosted experiences.

Information Sharing

User information is not intended to be sold. Information may be shared only as necessary to operate requested services, support authorized account connections, comply with legal obligations, or protect platform security and integrity.

Support And Questions

Users can request support or ask privacy-related questions by contacting ArdonyxApps@gmail.com.

Public-facing policy summary

This page is intended to provide a clear public summary of Ardonyx Finance privacy practices. If retention periods, jurisdiction-specific disclosures, or additional legal obligations apply later, this policy should be expanded accordingly.
